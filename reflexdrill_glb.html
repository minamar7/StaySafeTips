📖 High Block Technique

  • Ready Position: Stand in natural stance with arms relaxed at sides
  • Initiation: Begin raising blocking arm straight upward
  • Execution: Drive arm up with explosive power, elbow bending naturally
  • Final Position: Fist above and forward of head, elbow bent ~90°, forearm protecting head
  • Guard: Other arm maintains defensive position or pulls to hip (hikite)
  • Return: Lower arm smoothly back to ready position
