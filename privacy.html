Stay Safe Tips Elite is committed to your safety and privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the application environment.

1. Privacy & Zero Data Collection

We do not require user accounts. We do not collect, store, or share personal identifiers, names, or contact information on external servers.

2. SOS Hub & Location Services

The SOS Hub and Hospital Locator features require access to your location to function:

  • On-Device Processing: Your location is used strictly in real-time on your device to find nearby help.
  • No Tracking: Location data is never transmitted to us or any third party, and no location history is recorded.

3. Local Storage & Progress

Your Dojo achievements, XP, and badges are stored locally on your device. Clearing the app's cache or uninstalling it will permanently remove this data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Services and AdMob to support the app. These services may collect anonymous technical data (e.g., device model, advertising ID) for analytics and ad delivery. Review their terms at the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Families & Children

Stay Safe Tips Elite is an educational tool for all ages. We strongly recommend that children and minors use the training modules under **parental supervision** to ensure techniques are practiced safely. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Permissions

  • Internet: To stream professional Dojo video lessons and receive threat radar updates.
  • Location: Strictly for emergency hospital searches via the SOS Hub.
