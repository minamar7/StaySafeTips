Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Stay Safe Elite, accessible from our GitHub Pages and mobile application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Stay Safe Elite and how we use it.

1. Local Data Storage

Stay Safe Elite does not require user registration. Your progress data, including XP, Levels, and Badges, is stored locally on your device (using LocalStorage). We do not have access to this data on our servers.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our app. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to Stay Safe Elite or other websites on the Internet.

  • Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • We do not control these cookies used by third-party advertisers.

3. External Links

Our application may contain links to external social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok). Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites.

4. Device Information (Audit Tool)

The "Elite Checkup" tool accesses basic device information (battery level, network status) in real-time. This information is processed only within your browser/app and is never transmitted, stored, or shared.

5. Consent

By using our app, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
