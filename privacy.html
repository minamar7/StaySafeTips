⚠️ Educational Disclaimer

The developers are not responsible for any injury, misuse, or legal consequences resulting from the application of this information.

Stay Safe Elite is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how users maintain control over their data.

1. No Account Required

Stay Safe Elite does not require user registration. We do not create user accounts and we do not operate backend servers that store personal profiles.

2. Local Data Storage

All progress data (XP, Levels, Badges, quiz results) is stored locally on your device using browser-based LocalStorage technology within the app environment.

  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not have access to your locally stored progress.
  • Data Deletion: Users may delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app cache/data from Android settings or uninstalling the application.

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob and Google AdSense to display advertisements. These services may automatically collect certain information for advertising and analytics purposes.

  • Android Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Device model and operating system version
  • IP address (used for approximate location detection)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostic and crash data (if provided by Google services)

This information is processed directly by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. Stay Safe Elite does not access, store, or control this third-party data.

Users may opt-out of personalized advertising through: Google Ads Settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Any data collected through advertising services is handled exclusively by Google as an independent data controller.

5. Data Retention

Stay Safe Elite does not retain personal user data on external servers. Any information collected by third-party advertising providers is retained according to their respective data retention policies.

6. On-Device Tools (Elite Checkup)

The “Elite Checkup” feature analyzes limited device metrics (such as battery level and network status) in real-time. All processing occurs locally on your device. No system data is transmitted, stored, or shared externally.

7. External Links

The app may contain links to external platforms (such as social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

8. Children’s Privacy

Stay Safe Elite is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. User Rights

Users maintain full control of their local data. Since we do not operate centralized user databases, there is no account-based data to access or delete beyond local device storage.

10. Consent

By using Stay Safe Elite, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.
