📖 High Block Technique - Tang Soo Do

  • Ready Stance: Natural stance, arms relaxed at sides
  • Rising Motion: Right arm rises diagonally in front of forehead, fist slightly rotated outward
  • Blocking Position: Fist positioned just in front of forehead, elbow bent at ~120° protecting the side of the head
  • Hikite (Chamber): Left arm pulls back strongly to the hip, fist at waist level, elbow at 90°
  • Body Rotation: Slight waist rotation for power generation, eyes forward
  • Return: Smooth return to starting position
